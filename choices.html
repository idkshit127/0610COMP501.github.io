Description: In this page, we will discuss the choices available to people within society regarding AI technology in cars. We will consider several factors that may determine the reasoning behind many people's decisions.

Availability: The decision to purchase an electric vehicle, like Tesla, remains limited for many individuals in today's society. The high prices of electric vehicles make them unaffordable for a significant portion of the population, and the lack of charging infrastructure further hinders their convenience. Additionally, as the majority still relies on gasoline-powered cars, the transition to electric vehicles is yet to become a mainstream choice for most consumers.

Options: Tesla offers a range of models, including the Tesla Model 3, Model S, Model X, and Model Y, providing different options to suit various needs and preferences. These models offer features such as impressive range capabilities, advanced autopilot features, innovative technology, and luxurious interiors. This allows the buyers to have various options of cars and models to choose from. With the variation of models, the buyers can choose models that can fit their budget's lifestyles, and technological preferences for those considering AI technology in cars.
